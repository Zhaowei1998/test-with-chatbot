A little bit about myself

I am currently working on my doctorate in Instrcutional Technology at the University of South Florida. I gained my master in Applied linguistics and TESOL from King's College London. I am interested in cognitive and affective issues in learning, language processing and acquisition among people with memory deficiency and learning difficulties, and how technology can be better utilized to address such isssues.

If you are interested in my project or have any questions, please email me at zhaowei@usf.edu  

USF Instructional Technology PhD program 

USF's Instructional Technology Program offers Master of Education, Educational Specialist, and Doctor of Philosophy degrees in Curriculum and Instruction with a concentraion in Instructional Technology. The program also offer a newly developed Master of Science in Learning Design and Technology. In addition, the program offers several graduate certificate programs including: Cybersecurity Awareness and Education eLearning Design and Development Florida Virtual/Digital Educator Web Design. 

Instructional Technology 

 Instructional technology is the practical technique of instructional delivery. It aims to provide effective learning through the use of digital or online technology for instruction, as well as the tools used to facilitate course design communication, research and interaction.
